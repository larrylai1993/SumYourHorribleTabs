Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Sum Your Horrible Tabs ("the extension") is a Chrome extension for tab management. This policy describes what data is handled and how.

Data collected

The extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data remains on your device.

The following information is stored locally in chrome.storage.local:

  • Gemini API Key — entered by the user, stored only in the local browser profile.
  • Selected model — the Gemini model preference.
  • Tab snapshots — URLs and titles of tabs the user explicitly chooses to save.
  • Group metadata — tab group names and colors created by the extension.
  • Theme preference — light or dark mode setting.

Data sent to third parties

When the user clicks "AI 智慧分組", the extension sends tab titles and domain names (not full URLs) to the Google Gemini API for grouping suggestions. No other data is sent externally. The Gemini API is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data not collected

  • Browsing history
  • Full page URLs or content
  • Any form of analytics or telemetry
  • Personally identifiable information

Permissions

  • tabs — to read tab titles and URLs for grouping and deduplication.
  • tabGroups — to create and manage Chrome tab groups.
  • storage — to save settings and snapshots locally.
  • contextMenus — to add a right-click shortcut for removing duplicate tabs.
