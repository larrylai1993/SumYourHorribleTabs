## Name
Sum Your Horrible Tabs

## Summary (132 chars max)
Organize your browser chaos with Gemini AI — auto-group, sort, deduplicate, and snapshot your tabs.

## Description (EN)

Do you have 80 tabs open and no idea what half of them are? Sum Your Horrible Tabs helps you take back control.

**AI Tab Grouping**
Paste your Gemini API key, click one button, and the extension analyzes your open tabs and groups them into logical categories — things like "Work", "Dev", "Shopping", "Videos". Groups are named and color-coded automatically. Supports all Gemini models including Gemini 2.5 Flash.

**Tab Sorting**
Sort all tabs alphabetically by title, or cluster them by domain so related sites sit together.

**Duplicate Detection**
Scans for duplicate tabs automatically. Removes them individually or all at once. Also available from the right-click menu on the extension icon.

**Tab Snapshots**
Save the current state of your tabs (including group structure) and restore them later. Useful before closing a work session you want to return to. Up to 5 snapshots.

**Group Management**
Rename groups and change their colors directly from the extension panel.

**Dark Mode**
Follows your system preference, or toggle manually.

---

**Getting a Gemini API Key**
Get one for free at https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey — the free tier is sufficient for most usage.

**Privacy**
Your API key and tab data never leave your browser except to call the Gemini API for grouping. Nothing is collected or sent to any other server. See the full privacy policy for details.

## Description (ZH-TW)

你是不是開了 80 個分頁，卻不知道其中一半是什麼？Sum Your Horrible Tabs 幫你把分頁整理好。

**AI 智慧分組**
貼上 Gemini API Key，按一個按鈕，擴充功能就會分析你的分頁並自動歸類成工作、開發、購物、影音等群組，自動命名並附上顏色。支援所有 Gemini 模型。

**分頁排序**
依名稱字母排序，或依網域排序，讓相關分頁聚在一起。

**重複分頁偵測**
自動掃描重複分頁，可單獨關閉或一鍵清除。也可透過右鍵選單快速清除。

**分頁快照**
儲存當前所有分頁（含群組結構），之後可以還原。適合在結束一個工作 session 前備份。最多儲存 5 個快照。

**群組管理**
在面板中直接重新命名群組或更改顏色。

**深色模式**
跟隨系統設定，也可手動切換。
